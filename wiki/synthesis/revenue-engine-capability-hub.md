# Revenue Engine Capability Hub

> [verified 2026-06-12] A-Wiki revenue lane turns private knowledge into public-safe offers, content, and product experiments by starting from evidence and validating claims before publishing.

## Route

| Step | Tooling | Done when |
|---|---|---|
| Source | Local wiki search, source pages, user-provided evidence | Claims trace back to wiki/source/user input |
| Package | `awiki-creator-layer`, Thai/social skills, render-html review surfaces | Draft has audience, offer, channel, and source basis |
| Validate | Web/latest research when trend, pricing, or market claims matter | Sources include real URLs and retrieval date |
| Publish-ready | Privacy scan and public-safe rewrite | No customer data, secrets, private path, or unsupported metric |

## Safety Gate

- Voice profile, drafts, analytics, and private business notes live in `drive/personal/creator/`.
- Do not invent revenue numbers, market size, case studies, or personal stories.
- Use the Cost Pyramid: local search first, free/cheap research second, primary agent validates.
